The Problem

Streaming services are convenient — until they aren't. Songs disappear when licenses expire, offline mode costs extra, your listening data is sold, and you never truly own anything. For people who've built real music collections over years, that's a raw deal.

  • No offline access without a premium subscription
  • Songs removed without warning when labels pull deals
  • $10–$20 per month, forever, just to listen
  • Your listening habits tracked and sold to advertisers
  • No custom organization — the algorithm decides your library structure

If you have thousands of local MP3s sitting in folders, you deserve a player that treats them like the collection they are — not an afterthought.

The Solution

LidaMixPlay is a free, open-source desktop music manager built for people who own their music. It runs entirely on your machine — no internet required, no account, no tracking.

🗂️ Smart Genre Library

Create custom genres and map local folders to them. Your structure, your rules — not an algorithm's.

🎤 Live Synced Lyrics

Real-time karaoke-style lyrics that highlight line by line as the song plays, fetched from ID3 tags or auto-retrieved.

📊 Audio Visualizer

4 stunning visual modes — bars, waves, circular halos, and particle pulses — that react live to your music.

⚡ Instant Quick Play

Drag and drop any audio file to play it immediately — no import needed. Perfect for one-off tracks.

🎚️ Crossfade & Speed

Silky smooth crossfade between tracks and adjustable playback speed from 0.5× to 2.0×. DJ-grade control.

📅 Listening History

Every track you play is logged on a heatmap calendar. Your personal music journal, built right in.

🌙 Dark & Light Themes

Two carefully crafted themes — dark and light — both complete and beautiful. Switch with one click.

🔒 100% Private & Offline

No accounts, no cloud uploads, no analytics. Your music never leaves your machine. Ever.

How It Works

⚡ Zero Setup, Instant Play

Download, extract, and open. No installer wizard, no account creation, no cloud sync. Point LidaMixPlay at your music folders, assign genres, and your library is ready in seconds.

🖥️ Runs 100% Locally

Built on Electron — the same framework behind VS Code and Slack — LidaMixPlay is a true native desktop app. Everything happens on your machine. No internet connection is ever required to play, organize, or enjoy your library.

📤 Portable Config

Export your entire library structure to JSON and import it on any machine. M3U playlist import is also supported. Your setup travels with you.
