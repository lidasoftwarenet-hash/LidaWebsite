About LiDa Software

LiDa Software started as a small side project by Hen Faibish, a developer who just couldnt stop building things, even on weekends. Its not a company with investors, slogans, or slides full of buzzwords. Its just one guy who really loves code and believes good ideas should be shared, not locked behind paywalls or jargon. LiDa creates small, useful, sometimes weird tools... the kind that make developers smile, save time, or say oh, thats actually clever. No hype. No AI drama. Just clean code, curiosity, and the joy of making things work (eventually). When Hen isnt coding, hes probably testing another idea, writing about software, or explaining to his kids why just five more minutes of debugging can actually mean an hour.

Motto: build cool stuff, keep it simple, and share what you learn.
